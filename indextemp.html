About

A lifetime through a lens.

After 150 weddings, and an Award for Wedding photographer of the year I decided I wanted to focus on my dream of becoming a newborn photographer. I decided to specialize in the creative artistic side of newborn photography and I am constantly in awe of the perfection and beauty of capturing sleeping babies.

What's in my bag?

I am a very loyal Nikon girl.

I have a Nikon D5 , Nikon D3s and Nikon D700. I love my Nikon 50mm 1.4 prime lens and Nikon 60mm macro lens which I never leave home without. My Nikon 70-200mm f2.8 is my dream portrait lens for outdoor sessions!
